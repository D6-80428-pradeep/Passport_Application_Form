Documents Required for Tatkaal Applications

For obtaining passport under Tatkaal scheme, along with the address, birth and non-ECR proofs (as applicable) applicants over the age of 18 years have to submit any three of the documents specified in the below-mentioned List of Acceptable Documents and applicants below the age of 18 years have to submit any two of the documents from Serial numbers 1 to 6 specified in the below-mentioned List of Acceptable Documents.

List of Acceptable Documents

  • PVC Aadhaar Card/Complete Original Aadhaar Letter/ e-Aadhaar Card with digital signature verified mark issued by UIDAI or Aadhaar Card (Small cut-out Aadhaar card/smart card printed by non-UIDAI entities will not be accepted.)
  • Permanent Account Number (PAN) Card
  • Student Photo Identity Cards issued by Recognized Educational Institutions
  • Birth Certificates issued under the Registration of Births and Deaths Act, 1969 (18 of 1969)
  • Ration Card (latest or updated)
  • Last Passport issued (in case of re-issue only)
  • Electors Photo Identity Card (EPIC)
  • Driving Licenses (valid and within the jurisdiction of the State of submission of applicant)
  • Bank Passbook or Kisan Passbook or Post Office Passbook (with applicant’s photo attested and with the latest transaction updated)
  • Scheduled Caste or Scheduled Tribe or Other backward class Certificate
  • Arms License issued under the Arms Act, 1959 (54 of 1959)
  • Pension Documents such as ex-servicemen's Pension book or Pension payment order issued to retired government employees, ex-servicemen's Widow or Dependent Certificates, Old Age Pension Order
  • Service Photo Identity Card issued by State/Central Government, Public Sector Undertakings, local bodies, or Public Limited Companies

List of Ineligible Categories

  • Major change in name
  • Those with present address outside jurisdiction of the RPO
  • Minor’s application with Annexure C (Ex. Minors whose Parents are divorced or separated, etc)
  • Applicants repatriated from abroad at Government cost
  • Applicants deported to India or travelled on Emergency Certificate
  • Persons of Naga origin including minors (below 18 years)
  • Persons of Naga origin residing outside Nagaland
  • Persons of J&K origin including minors (below 18 years)
  • Applicants who attract section 6(2)(e) or 6(2)(f) (related to criminal court cases)
  • Applicants with adverse police report, put under watch list, habitual passport losers or who had earlier travelled on forged or fake passports

Fresh Issue Cases

  • Citizens of India by Registration/Naturalization (granted citizenship by MHA)
  • Children adopted by Indian or Foreign parents
  • Child born out of wedlock and has single parent
  • Children born out of surrogacy
  • Minors with single parent

Re-issue Cases

  • Lost/stolen passport cases
  • Re-issue of Short Validity Passport
  • Passport damaged beyond recognition cases
  • Change in sex, appearance or signature
  • Change/correction of date of birth or place of birth
  • Change of father/mother/spouse name

Undertaking for Tatkaal Scheme

Applicants applying under Tatkaal scheme need to fill the “Undertaking for Tatkaal Scheme”-Hindi / English.
